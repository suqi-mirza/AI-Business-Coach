● Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated April 8, 2026

1. What we collect

We collect only what we need to make Coachly useful: your account info (email, name), the business context you give us (company, stage, goals), and the conversations and metrics you choose to share.

2. How we use it

To run the product. That means generating recommendations, tracking your health score, and remembering context across sessions. We do not sell your data and we do not use your business content to train AI models.

3. Where it lives

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Production data sits in SOC-2 compliant infrastructure in the US and EU.

4. Your rights

Export everything, delete everything, anytime. Email privacy@coachly.ai and we'll handle it within 7 days.

5. Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies only. No third-party tracking, no ad pixels, no behavioral profiling.

6. Changes

If we ever update this policy, we'll email you before it takes effect. No surprises.
